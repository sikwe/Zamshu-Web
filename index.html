Our Statements

Working as a team to be Zambia’s leading, World class supplier of quality leather and leather-based products for the Benefit of Employees and Shareholders.

We exist to consistently produce and supply quality leather and leather products locally and internationally to customers’ specification and satisfaction by:

  • Processing leather and leather products using quality materials
  • Implementing cost effective and efficient resource management
  • Using committed, highly skilled, qualified and motivated workforce
  • Meeting customer requirements and specification
  • Sustaining a safe, healthy environment.
